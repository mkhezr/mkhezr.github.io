The Future of Tech

A couple of months back, I asked Todd Sieling “What is in the fucture of Tech?” He told me recently that he was asked the same question in an interview not long after our initial conversation.

In our five minute talk which follows, I asked the same question one more time. It was delightful to hear from Todd’s vision. It gets even more interesting when one sees the contrast of the ideas Todd talks about, with the dominant conversations about technology on the web. It reminds us once more to re-imagine the possibilities from a human standpoint.
