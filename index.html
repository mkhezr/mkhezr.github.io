Toxic Beauty

Toxic Beauty is an investigation into the use of harmful chemicals in beauty and personal care products. In this study, I analyze ingredients used by 1600 different brands. The analysis reveals a particular landscape of the beauty industry and is an invitation to know more and to choose wiser.

Plastic Fashion

Fashion industry is a top contributor to climate change. Moreover, a lack of transparency in the fashion supply chain, makes a fertile soil for modern slavery to grow. In this study I compare two groups of fashion brands. I look for indicators that would tell us if these brands are doing the right thing to address the environmental and social issues associated with their businesses.

About Maryam

I’m an explorer and feel lucky to have an extensive technology toolset to rely on. I hold a BSc. in Computer Science and an MSc. in Data Mining and AI. I’ve written code and designed software for more than a decade. Currently I’m focused on data discovery and analysis, community & language detection as well as writing visual essays. The promise of technology in my opinion, is a future in which we have understood and alleviated our (somewhat broken) relationship with nature and people.
